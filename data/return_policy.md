# Return Policy

We offer a 30-day return policy on all physical products purchased from our store.

## Eligible Items
- Unused products in original packaging
- Items returned within 30 days of delivery

## Non-Returnable Items
- Digital licenses and software downloads
- Personalized or custom-made products

## How to Return
1. Contact support@example.com with your order number
2. Receive a return authorization (RA) number
3. Ship the item back using the prepaid label we provide
4. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the item

## Refund Method
Refunds are issued to the original payment method. Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to a defect or our error.
