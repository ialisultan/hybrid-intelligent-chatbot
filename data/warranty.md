# Warranty Coverage

All SmartWidget products include a standard 2-year limited warranty covering manufacturing defects.

## What Is Covered
- Hardware failures under normal use
- Battery defects (FitTrack Band)
- Software bugs in CloudSync License for the subscription period

## What Is Not Covered
- Accidental damage, water damage, or misuse
- Normal wear and tear
- Unauthorized repairs or modifications
- Commercial use beyond stated limits

## How to Claim
Contact support with your proof of purchase and a description of the issue. We may request diagnostic logs or photos. Approved claims receive repair, replacement, or refund at our discretion.

## Extended Warranty
Customers may purchase an extended 3-year warranty at checkout for an additional fee.
