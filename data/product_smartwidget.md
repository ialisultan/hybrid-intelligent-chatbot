# SmartWidget Pro — Product Description

SmartWidget Pro is our flagship smart home hub for connecting and automating devices across your home.

## Key Features
- **Universal compatibility**: Works with Wi-Fi, Zigbee, and Matter devices
- **Voice control**: Integrates with major voice assistants
- **Automation engine**: Create rules like "turn off lights when leaving home"
- **Energy dashboard**: Track power usage across connected devices
- **Local processing**: Core automations run on-device for privacy and speed

## Specifications
- Processor: Quad-core ARM
- Connectivity: Wi-Fi 6, Bluetooth 5.2, Zigbee 3.0
- Storage: 8 GB internal
- Dimensions: 120 x 120 x 35 mm

## Setup
Download the SmartWidget app, plug in the hub, and follow the in-app pairing wizard. Most users complete setup in under 10 minutes.
